# Room Layout Tool — Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Blueprint / Technical Drawing
<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement**: Technical Blueprint / Architectural Drawing
**Core Principles**: Precision-first, grid-based, professional drafting aesthetic
**Color Philosophy**: Dark navy background with cyan/white grid lines — evokes CAD software and architectural blueprints. Accents in amber/gold for selected items.
**Layout Paradigm**: Full-screen canvas with a collapsible left panel for furniture library and a bottom info bar for dimensions/stats.
**Signature Elements**: Dotted grid overlay on canvas, dimension annotations with arrows, monospace typography for measurements.
**Interaction Philosophy**: Snap-to-grid by default, precise numeric input for placement, hover reveals dimension callouts.
**Animation**: Subtle fade-in for furniture placement, smooth snap animations, dimension lines animate on selection.
**Typography System**: "JetBrains Mono" for measurements/labels, "Inter" for UI chrome.
</idea>

## Approach 2: Clean Interior Design Studio
<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement**: Modern Interior Design Studio / Scandinavian Minimalism
**Core Principles**: Warm whites and natural tones, generous whitespace, tactile feel
**Color Philosophy**: Warm off-white (#FAF8F5) background, warm gray canvas floor, furniture in muted earth tones. Accent in deep terracotta or forest green.
**Layout Paradigm**: Left sidebar with furniture palette, large central canvas, right panel for selected item properties. Top toolbar for tools/actions.
**Signature Elements**: Soft drop shadows on furniture pieces, subtle wood-grain texture on floor, rounded furniture silhouettes.
**Interaction Philosophy**: Drag-and-drop feels physical and weighted, resize handles are visible and inviting.
**Animation**: Spring physics on drag release, gentle scale-up on hover, smooth panel transitions.
**Typography System**: "Playfair Display" for headings, "DM Sans" for UI labels and measurements.
</idea>

## Approach 3: Professional Floor Plan Tool (CHOSEN)
<response>
<idea>
**Design Movement**: Professional SaaS Tool / Clean Technical UI
**Core Principles**: High information density, clarity over decoration, functional elegance
**Color Philosophy**: Light gray (#F4F5F7) app background, crisp white canvas with subtle grid, furniture in distinct category colors (blues for seating, greens for storage, etc.). Primary accent in slate blue (#3B5BDB).
**Layout Paradigm**: Fixed left sidebar (furniture library + categories), full-height scrollable canvas in center, collapsible right panel for item properties. Sticky top bar with room stats and tools.
**Signature Elements**: Color-coded furniture categories, floor space heatmap overlay toggle, dimension rulers on canvas edges.
**Interaction Philosophy**: Drag from sidebar to place, click to select, handles to resize/rotate. Keyboard shortcuts for power users.
**Animation**: Smooth drag with real-time collision hints, selection ring pulse, panel slide transitions.
**Typography System**: "Space Grotesk" for headings and labels, "IBM Plex Mono" for dimension readouts.
</idea>
